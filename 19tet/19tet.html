This is an instrument in 19-tone equal temperament.
Press the piano keys or the keys on your keyboard to play it.
Press "↑" or "↓" to adjust the octave.
Press "←" or "→" to adjust the key.
Sound Mode Piano is just like a normal piano.
Sound Mode Strings makes the sound continuous.
Sound Mode Bells lets the sound throughout defaultly.
Pedal lets sound throughout in Sound Mode Piano and Strings, stops the sound in Bells.
