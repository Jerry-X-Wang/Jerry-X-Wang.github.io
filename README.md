## 19-tone Equal Temperament Instrument

This page is to make a 19-tone equal emperament instrument.

Press some keys to play sound.

Press ArrowUp or ArrowDown to change the Octave.

Press ArrowLeft or ArrowRight to change the Key.

## BPM Test

## Audio Visualizer

This page is to visualize an audio in your microphone or in your system sound, using FFT.

## Metronome

This page provides a online metronome.

Tap the beat dots to change its type.

MinBPM is 1 and MaxBPM is 5000, not only the 30 and 300 on the range bar.

BPM is exactly accurate, unless your device is laggy.
